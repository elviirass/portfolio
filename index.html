DESIGN PROCESS

My design process

As a designer, my goal is always to understand, empathize and listen to the end-user. The right set of design tools depends on the problem at hand. It's not a guessing game nor magic, but it all starts with a purpose. That purpose might be a business interest, an improvement idea, or just a feeling that we could do better.

I love the much-used double diamond model in service design as it accurately describes the rollercoaster you are about to hop on. First, the goal is to 'design the right things', i.e. to find the real painpoints that are to be improved in the next phase. Then, you 'design the things right' - easier said than done, but it can be achieved by collaborating with the users and iterating with different types of prototypes.

Finally, you have a solution that really fulfills customer needs, adds value to the business, and is possible to develop.
